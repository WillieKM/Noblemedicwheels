Our Mission: Your Safety and Comfort

Noble Medic Wheels was founded on the simple belief that everyone deserves access to safe, reliable, and dignified transportation. We are a locally-owned service dedicated to serving our community in the **greater Seattle-Tacoma area**. We know that mobility can be a challenge, but it should never be a barrier to receiving medical care or staying connected with your community.

Our team is more than just drivers; we are trained professionals who are background-checked, insured, and committed to providing a **compassionate, punctual, and stress-free** experience. From the moment you book a ride to the moment you arrive safely at your destination, we are here for you.

How We Can Help (Our Services)

We provide transportation for a wide variety of non-emergency needs. Our fleet is clean, comfortable, and equipped to handle your specific mobility requirements.

  • **Medical Appointments:** Doctor's visits, dialysis, physical therapy, rehabilitation, etc.
  • **Hospital Discharge:** A safe and comfortable ride home from the hospital or care facility.
  • **Pharmacy & Errands:** We can take you to the pharmacy, grocery store, or bank.
  • **Social & Family Visits:** Stay connected with your loved ones and community.
  • **Wheelchair & Ambulatory Service:** Our vehicles are fully accessible and our drivers are trained in safe patient assistance.
